Equipment Specifications - Harvest & Clarification

‚öôÔ∏è Disk-Stack Centrifuge

  • Model: Alfa Laval BTPX 205 or Carr Powerfuge
  • Speed: 8,000-10,000 RPM
  • Flow Rate: 200-400 L/hr
  • Function: Remove cells, cell debris (>5Œºm)
  • Output: Clarified supernatant (turbidity <50 NTU)
  • CIP/SIP: Automated cleaning/sterilization

üìÑ Depth Filtration

  • System: Pall SUPRAcap or Merck Millistak+
  • Configuration: Dual-stage (0.6Œºm ‚Üí 0.2Œºm)
  • Technology: Single-use capsule filters
  • Capacity: 200-400 kg cell mass/m¬≤
  • Function: Remove fine particulates, DNA, HCP
  • Output: Crystal-clear filtrate (turbidity <5 NTU)

üîß Harvest Skid Components

  • Transfer Pumps: Peristaltic (Watson Marlow)
  • Hold Tanks: 1,000-2,000L single-use bags
  • Sensors: Pressure, flow, conductivity, pH
  • Control: DCS/SCADA integration
  • Materials: 316L SS or single-use biocontainers

Equipment Specifications - Chromatography Systems

üß≤ Protein A Affinity Chromatography

  • Resin: GE MabSelect SuRe, Poros MabCapture
  • Column: 300L (60 cm diameter √ó 30 cm bed)
  • System: GE AKTA Ready or Cytiva BioProcess
  • Load: 30-50 g mAb/L resin (dynamic binding)
  • Elution: Low pH (3.0-3.5) citrate buffer
  • Yield: >95% recovery, >98% purity

‚ûï Cation Exchange (CEX)

  • Resin: Poros 50 XS, GE Capto S ImpRes
  • Mode: Bind-elute
  • Column: 100L (40 cm √ó 20 cm bed)
  • Function: Remove aggregates, HCP, leached Protein A
  • Elution: Salt gradient (0-500 mM NaCl)
  • Output: Monomer purity >99%

‚ûñ Anion Exchange (AEX)

  • Resin: Poros 50 HQ, GE Q Sepharose FF
  • Mode: Flow-through (mAb in flow-through)
  • Column: 100L (40 cm √ó 20 cm bed)
  • Function: Remove DNA, viruses, endotoxin, HCP
  • Binding: Impurities bind, product flows through
  • Output: DNA <100 pg/dose, HCP <100 ppm

üíª Chromatography Automation

  • System: GE AKTA Ready, Cytiva BioProcess
  • Control: UNICORN software, DeltaV integration
  • Sensors: UV280, conductivity, pH, pressure
  • Fraction Collection: Automated based on UV signal
  • CIP/SIP: Automated sequences (NaOH, sanitization)
  • Data: Electronic batch records, 21 CFR Part 11

Equipment Specifications - Viral Clearance

‚öóÔ∏è Viral Inactivation (Low pH)

  • Method: Low pH hold (citrate buffer)
  • Conditions: pH 3.5, 60 minutes, 18-25¬∞C
  • Hold Tank: 500-1,000L jacketed vessel
  • Monitoring: pH probe, temperature, timer
  • Log Reduction: >4 logs for enveloped viruses
  • Validation: Spiking studies with model viruses

ü¶† Nanofiltration (Viral Removal)

  • Filter: Asahi Planova 20N (20 nm pores)
  • Mechanism: Size exclusion (virus removal)
  • Format: Single-use cartridge
  • Capacity: 200-500 L/m¬≤
  • Log Reduction: >4-6 logs (MuLV, MMV, PPV)
  • Integrity Test: Bubble point test before/after

üìä Viral Clearance Strategy

  • Orthogonal Steps: Low pH + nanofiltration
  • Regulatory: >12 log reduction required (FDA)
  • Model Viruses: MuLV, MMV, X-MuLV
  • Testing: TCID50, qPCR, infectivity assays
  • Documentation: Viral validation reports

Equipment Specifications - Concentration & Formulation

üîÑ Ultrafiltration/Diafiltration (UF/DF)

  • System: Merck Pellicon 3, Sartorius Sartocon
  • Membrane: 30 kDa MWCO (polyethersulfone)
  • Area: 2-10 m¬≤ (depends on scale)
  • Mode: TFF (tangential flow filtration)
  • Function: 10-30X concentration + buffer exchange
  • Final: 50-150 mg/mL protein concentration

üíä Formulation System

  • Tank: 200-500L jacketed vessel
  • Components: Histidine, sucrose, polysorbate 80
  • pH Adjustment: HCl or NaOH addition
  • Mixing: Gentle agitation (avoid foaming)
  • Monitoring: pH, conductivity, concentration (UV280)
  • Target: pH 5.5-6.5, osmolality 280-320 mOsm/kg

‚ú® Sterile Filtration

  • Filter: Sartorius Sartopore 2, Pall Supor
  • Pore Size: 0.2Œºm (dual redundant)
  • Format: Single-use cartridge
  • Capacity: 200-500 L/m¬≤
  • Integrity Test: Bubble point/diffusion test
  • Function: Terminal bioburden reduction (<10 CFU)

üßä Drug Substance Storage

  • Container: 10-50L single-use bags or SS tanks
  • Material: EVA, polyethylene (extractables tested)
  • Storage: -80¬∞C to -20¬∞C (frozen) or 2-8¬∞C
  • Stability: 18-24 months at -20¬∞C
  • Monitoring: Temperature, fill volume, integrity

Analytical Testing - In-Process Controls

üî¨ Purity Analysis

  • SEC-HPLC: Aggregates, fragments, monomer %
  • SDS-PAGE: Reducing/non-reducing purity
  • CE-SDS: Automated capillary electrophoresis
  • Imaged CE: Protein Express system
  • Target: >99% monomer, <2% HMWS, <1% LMWS

üß™ Impurity Testing

  • HCP ELISA: Host cell proteins (<100 ppm)
  • DNA (qPCR): Residual DNA (<100 pg/dose)
  • Protein A ELISA: Leached ligand (<100 ppm)
  • Endotoxin (LAL): <0.5 EU/mg protein
  • Bioburden: <10 CFU before sterile filtration

üìà Product Quality

  • Protein Concentration: UV280, BCA, or HPLC
  • pH: Benchtop pH meter (target ¬±0.2)
  • Osmolality: Freezing point depression
  • Appearance: Visual inspection (clarity, color)
  • Particulates: HIAC or light obscuration

‚≠ê Release Testing Instruments

  • HPLC/UPLC: Agilent 1290, Waters Acquity
  • Mass Spec: LC-MS/MS for glycan analysis
  • Capillary Electrophoresis: Sciex PA800
  • Spectrophotometer: Nanodrop, Cary UV-Vis
  • Osmometer: Advanced Instruments 2020

üéØ Key Success Factors

  • Step Yield Optimization: Target >90% per step for overall >50% yield
  • Purity Progression: Each step increases purity (98% ‚Üí 99% ‚Üí 99.5% ‚Üí >99.5%)
  • Viral Safety: Two orthogonal clearance mechanisms (low pH + nanofiltration) = >12 log reduction
  • Process Validation: 3 consecutive commercial batches demonstrating consistency
  • Resin Lifetime: Protein A: 100-200 cycles, CEX/AEX: 50-100 cycles
  • Single-Use Technology: Depth filters, nanofilters, UF cassettes, bags reduce cleaning validation
  • Automation: AKTA systems with UNICORN software ensure reproducibility
  • Data Integrity: Electronic batch records, automated fraction collection, integrated LIMS
